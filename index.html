About Uplift

At Uplift Music and Uplift Experiences, we harness the transformative power of music, art, and comprehensive coaching to inspire, uplift, and grow individuals and communities. Our platform serves as a dynamic hub for music education, offering resources for teachers, composers, musicians, and bands to showcase and sell their digital prints, arrangements, and new compositions. Additionally, we provide artists with a marketplace to sell their arts and crafts. Our diverse coaching channels, including life coaching, arts therapies, and creative workshops, aim to nurture personal development. By empowering individuals with an accessible platform to sell their intellectual property and creations, we believe the individual becomes the catalyst for positive change, growth and economic development in their communities. Uplift Music and Uplift Experiences: creating a harmonious blend of artistic expression and personal advancement.

Who am I?

Graham Diesel has a post-graduate Music Education qualification and a Higher Teaching Diploma. He is a certified Neuro-Vocal Coach and his teaching is influenced by neuroscience, the body-mind connection and the Alexander technique. He has 25 years of experience teaching in a variety of settings.

Graham is passionate about changing lives through the arts. He dabbles in Music Production, composes and performs, and is an entrepreneur in the Arts and Education fields. loves art - he draws and paints in his spare time, and he loves hiking and going to the gym.

He is the father of two amazing boys.
